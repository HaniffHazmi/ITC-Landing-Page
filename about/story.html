Our Story

UTHM ITC Club is the official club under Fakulti Sains Komputer dan Teknologi Maklumat (FSKTM), UTHM. We serve as the main student body responsible for organizing events, workshops, and initiatives that empower students to be more active, experienced, and knowledgeable in the field of technology.

Our Vision

To cultivate a community of tech-aware and career-ready students in FSKTM through inclusive, hands-on activities and professional development initiatives.

Our Focus

Kelab ITC focuses on four key areas:

  • Community Engagement: Encouraging active participation and collaboration among peers.
  • Technical Skills: Hosting coding sessions, competitions, and tool-based workshops.
  • Industry Awareness: Inviting speakers and organizing expos to connect with professionals.
  • Student Development: Helping members build confidence and soft skills through events.
